## The Three Steps

### Step 1: Create

Describe the website you want. Be specific — include the type of site, colors, mood, and sections.

> Example: "A landing page for a boutique candle company called Ember & Glow. Warm amber and cream color palette, elegant fonts, sections for featured products, our story, and a newsletter signup."

Click **"Send to Claude"** and watch the AI write your site in real time.

### Step 2: Customize

Click the **"2. Customize"** button. Now describe what you'd like to change about your existing site.

> Example: "Make the color palette darker and more luxurious. Add a customer testimonials section. Make the fonts bolder."

The AI reads your current site and modifies it — no starting over.

### Step 3: Go Wild

Click the **"3. Go Wild"** button. This tells the AI to add advanced visual effects to your site — animations, particle effects, parallax scrolling, and more.

> Example: "Add floating ember particles, smooth scroll animations, and a parallax hero section."

---

## Tips for Great Results

- **Be specific.** "A modern tech startup site with dark mode, neon accents, and an animated hero" beats "a website."
- **Describe the feeling.** Words like "elegant," "playful," "bold," "minimalist," or "retro" guide the AI's design choices.
- **Mention sections.** Tell the AI what content blocks you want: hero, features, testimonials, pricing, contact, footer.
- **Name colors.** "Deep navy and gold" gives better results than "blue and yellow."
- **One request at a time.** Each step sends one prompt. Make it count.

## Quick Troubleshooting

- **Nothing happening?** Make sure you clicked "Send to Claude" and wait 30-60 seconds.
- **Got an error?** Click your current step button again and re-submit.
- **Want to start over?** Click "1. Create" and submit a new prompt.
