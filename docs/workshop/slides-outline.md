## Slide 3: What Is Agentic Coding?

**Traditional AI:** You ask a question, it gives an answer.

**Agentic AI:** You describe what you want, it *builds* it — writing real code, making design decisions, iterating on the result.

*Speaker notes: "You may have used ChatGPT to answer questions or write emails. Agentic coding tools go further — they don't just advise, they act. They write complete, working applications. The AI isn't assisting a programmer. It IS the programmer. You're the creative director."*

## Slide 5: What This Means

The new skill isn't **writing code**.

It's **directing AI** — knowing what to ask for, how to describe what you want, and when to iterate.

*Speaker notes: "Think about what happened when spreadsheets arrived. You didn't need to be a mathematician to model a business. The tool handled the math. We're at that same inflection point for software. The tool handles the code. You handle the vision."*

## Slide 8: Tips for Great Prompts

Be specific. Describe the **feeling**, not just the function.

| Instead of... | Try... |
|---------------|--------|
| "A restaurant website" | "A modern Japanese restaurant site with dark backgrounds, warm lighting photography feel, elegant serif fonts, sections for menu, reservations, and our story" |
| "Make it better" | "Change to a midnight blue and gold palette, add smooth scroll animations, make the typography more dramatic" |

*Speaker notes: Show this briefly. "The more specific you are, the better the result. Treat the AI like a talented designer who needs a clear brief." Then transition to hands-on.*
