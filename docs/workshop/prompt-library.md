## Step 2: Customize

Use these to modify the site you built in Step 1. Pick one or combine ideas.

### Change the Mood

> Make the entire design feel more premium and luxurious. Switch to a black and gold color palette, use thinner elegant fonts, add more whitespace between sections, and make all transitions slower and smoother.

> Transform this into a bold, energetic design. Bright saturated colors, oversized headings, playful asymmetric layouts, and snappy hover animations. Make it feel like it has personality.

> Make this feel more calm and minimal. Reduce the color palette to two colors plus white. Remove decorative elements. Increase whitespace dramatically. Use a single clean sans-serif font throughout.

### Add Content

> Add a "Meet the Team" section with four team member cards in a grid. Each card should have a placeholder avatar circle, name, role title, and a short bio. Use subtle hover effects that lift the card.

> Add a FAQ section with at least 6 questions and answers in an accordion-style layout that expands on click. Style it to match the existing design.

> Add a detailed pricing section with three tiers (Basic, Pro, Enterprise) in card format. Include feature lists with checkmarks, highlighted recommended tier, and call-to-action buttons.

### Redesign Elements

> Completely redesign the hero section. Make it full-screen height with a bold gradient background, centered oversized text with a typewriter animation effect, and a floating scroll-down indicator.

> Redesign the navigation. Make it a sticky glassmorphism navbar that becomes more opaque on scroll. Add smooth scroll to anchor links for each section.

> Redesign all buttons on the site. Make them larger, add gradient backgrounds, rounded corners, a subtle shadow, and a satisfying hover animation (scale up slightly with a glow effect).

## Step 3: Go Wild

These prompts push the AI to add advanced visual effects. The AI already knows to go big — your prompt guides the direction.

### Particle & Animation Effects

> Add a canvas-based particle system to the hero section — floating glowing dots that drift slowly and connect with faint lines when near each other. Add smooth parallax scrolling to all sections. Every section should fade and slide in as you scroll to it.

> Add a dynamic gradient background that slowly shifts colors across the entire page. Include floating geometric shapes (triangles, circles, hexagons) that rotate and drift. Add a scroll progress bar at the top of the page.

### Interactive & 3D

> Add 3D perspective tilt effects to all cards — they should subtly rotate toward the mouse cursor on hover. Add a ripple effect on all button clicks. Sections should have a staggered reveal animation on scroll where child elements animate in one after another.

> Add a mouse-following spotlight effect on the hero section — a soft radial gradient that follows the cursor. Include animated counters that count up when scrolled into view. Add a smooth page transition feel with section snap scrolling.

### Maximum Spectacle

> Go all out. Add particle constellations in the background, parallax layers at different scroll speeds, text that glows and pulses subtly, cards that flip in 3D on hover, a gradient aurora effect in the hero, smooth scroll-triggered animations on every single element, and a custom animated cursor.

> Transform this into a cinematic experience. Full-screen sections with scroll-snap. Hero section with animated text that types itself out letter by letter. Floating 3D elements with CSS transforms. Sections that reveal with dramatic slide and fade animations. A dark, immersive, high-contrast design with neon accent glows.

## Quick-Start Combos

If your group wants a fast, impressive result, use these three prompts in sequence:

### Combo A: "The Startup"
1. **Create:** "A SaaS landing page for a project management tool called FlowState. Dark mode, electric blue accents, modern sans-serif fonts. Hero with value prop, features grid, testimonials, pricing table, and CTA."
2. **Customize:** "Add a how-it-works section with 4 numbered steps. Make the pricing cards more dramatic with a glowing border on the featured plan. Add client logos."
3. **Go Wild:** "Add floating geometric particles, parallax scrolling, animated counters for stats, and 3D hover tilt on all cards."

### Combo B: "The Portfolio"
1. **Create:** "A photographer portfolio. Pure black background, minimal white text, full-width image placeholder sections. Thin elegant serif fonts. Gallery, about, contact."
2. **Customize:** "Add a dramatic full-screen hero with a single word in massive text. Add smooth scroll navigation. Make the gallery a responsive masonry grid."
3. **Go Wild:** "Add image reveal animations on scroll, a cursor-following light effect, parallax depth layers, and cinematic fade transitions between sections."

### Combo C: "The Restaurant"
1. **Create:** "An upscale Italian restaurant called Osteria Luna. Deep navy and warm gold palette. Elegant serif headings. Sections: hero with tagline, menu highlights, wine selection, private events, reservations, and location."
2. **Customize:** "Add a rotating seasonal specials banner. Include a chef's story section with a large quote. Make the menu section use a two-column layout with decorative dividers."
3. **Go Wild:** "Add candlelight-style flickering glow effects, smooth parallax on the hero, floating gold particle dust, elegant scroll animations that reveal each section with a fade-up, and a subtle background texture."
